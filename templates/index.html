How to Play

Draft a five-man roster from prospects whose names are hidden. You only see their college numbers — figure out who's who.
The Goal

Fill all five slots: QB, RB, two WR, and a defender. The best roster wins.

The Cards

Each card shows a prospect's position, college conference, measurables, and his final college season — but not his name. The skill is recognizing who the stat line belongs to.

Making a Pick

It's a snake draft. A prospect's name is revealed the moment you pick him. No take-backs.

Scoring

Each pick gets a letter grade against everyone at his position — A is elite, C is average. Your team score adds up how all five careers turned out; the highest total wins.

Modes

Daily — one board for everyone, ranked against the field. Play with Friends — draft live online, or pass one screen around.
