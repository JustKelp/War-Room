Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-12

WarRoom is a free NFL scouting-draft game. We collect as little as possible — here is exactly what that means.

What we collect

  • Account (optional): a username and a password. Passwords are stored only as a salted one-way hash — we never store or see your actual password.
  • Gameplay: your draft results and daily scores, so we can show your standing, history, and streak.
  • We do not collect email addresses, real names, location, or any other personal information, and we do not use advertising or third-party tracking.

How we use it

Only to run the game — to log you in, score your drafts, and rank the daily board. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone.

How it's stored

Data is kept in our own database on our server. Passwords are hashed. The site is served over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection.

Deleting your data

You can delete your account at any time from the account menu in the game. Deleting your account removes your login and detaches your results.

Children

WarRoom is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Player data

Football statistics shown in the game are factual public sports data used for an informational game; they do not concern you as a user.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy; material changes will be noted here. Questions can be directed to the site operator.
